Playful design and powerful code.


Umbrella Weather is a fun weather app powered by OpenWeatherMap APIs. It uses geocoding to display the user's local weather conditions, as well as a five day forecast. Umbrella Weather saves the user's favorite locations and sends gentle reminders to pack an umbrella when it's raining.
